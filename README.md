This B2B Medical Wholesale Platform is a high-performance E-commerce solution built with Django, specifically engineered for bulk distribution of surgical equipment and medical disposables. Featuring a clean, modern UI, the platform streamlines the supply chain by offering dynamic wholesale pricing, bulk inquiry management, and a robust product catalog designed for high-volume transactions. Now live on a professional .com domain, it provides medical wholesalers with a secure, scalable, and GST-compliant digital storefront to manage professional healthcare orders with ease.
